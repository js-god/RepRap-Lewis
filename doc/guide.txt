#Release#
Alpha:
	Major feature lock, major goals on roadmap met. 
Beta:
	Minor(aesthetic) goals may be added, bug fixes. Documentation begins.
Release Candidate:
	Critical bug fixes corrected, documentation updates complete.
Release:
	Bug fixes go to next version, documentation corrections continue. 


#Version system#
.xx
Tenths position, major revision, breaks part relations across series.
Hundreths position, minor revision, extensions, bug fixes, does not break part relations
Hundreths position >5, extensions, bug fixes, that break part relations
